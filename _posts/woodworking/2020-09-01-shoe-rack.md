* Wood glue
* Minwax pre-stain wood conditioner
* Minwax wood finish, Penetraiting stain, Semi-transparent - Red Mahogany 225
* Helmsmann spar urethane (spray) - clear semi-gloss indoor/outdoor

## Notes
