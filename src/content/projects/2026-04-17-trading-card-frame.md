![Trading Card Frame](/assets/img/2026-04-17-trading-card-frame/cardFrame_10.jpg)

- Download files on [Thingiverse](https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:7339232)
- Frame for displaying collectible trading or sports cards
- Fits the [BCW Magnetic Card holder](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B08BSVQ1HR)
- Frame is held to the back by [8mm x 2mm round magnets](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0F4CTQCKB)
- Magnets are also embedded along the edges so multiple frames can attach together for a larger display
- Magnets are embedded directly in the 3D print with pauses to allow insertion; align polarities consistently
- Frame pauses at 9.8 mm and 10.2 mm; back pauses at 2.8 mm

- Cherry frame built around the 3D printed frames with Padauk splines
- 3D printed frames allow for an "extra" padding on each edge to allow for the recess in the wood frame
