## Supplies

- 2x8 pine
- 2x6 treated pine
- 3/4" plywood
- Floor adhesive
- Screws
- Sound insulation
- Electrical wire
- In-floor electrical outlets

## Design

- Design was based on [this similar design](https://rogueengineer.com/diy-theater-riser-construction/) from Rogue Engineer ([video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0CzGENaTBAo))
