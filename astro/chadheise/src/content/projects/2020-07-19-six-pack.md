## Supplies

- 1x6 pine
- 7/8" wood dowel
- 1/4" baltic birch plywood
- Minwax pre-stain wood conditioner
- Minwax wood finish, Penetraiting stain, Semi-transparent - Provincial 211
- Minwax fast-drying polyurethane, Clear semi-gloss
